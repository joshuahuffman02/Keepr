## Introduction

The camping industry has seen remarkable growth and change. Understanding current trends helps you position your campground for success.

---

## Key 2024 Trends

### 1. Continued Growth

- Camping participation remains elevated post-pandemic
- New campers continue entering the market
- Multi-generational camping increasing

### 2. Technology Expectations Rising

- Online booking now expected, not optional
- WiFi quality influences booking decisions
- Mobile-first booking behavior
- Digital communication preferred

### 3. Experience Over Basics

- Glamping and unique accommodations growing
- Events and programming valued
- Local experiences sought
- Instagram-worthy elements matter

### 4. Sustainability Matters

- Eco-conscious travelers increasing
- Interest in campground sustainability practices
- Electric vehicle charging becoming expected
- Waste reduction and recycling noticed

### 5. Remote Work Integration

- "Workamping" and extended stays
- Strong WiFi as business requirement
- Quiet daytime environments valued
- Weekly/monthly rate demand

## Market Segments to Watch

| Segment        | Trend      | Opportunity            |
| -------------- | ---------- | ---------------------- |
| Young families | Growing    | Kid-friendly amenities |
| Remote workers | Emerging   | WiFi, extended stays   |
| Solo travelers | Increasing | Safety, community      |
| Pet owners     | Large      | Pet-friendly policies  |

---

## Implications for Campground Owners

1. Invest in technology (booking, WiFi)
2. Consider unique/glamping options
3. Develop events and experiences
4. Address sustainability visibly
5. Support longer stays

---

## Key Takeaways

- Camping industry remains strong
- Technology expectations are rising
- Experiences differentiate campgrounds
- Sustainability increasingly influences decisions
