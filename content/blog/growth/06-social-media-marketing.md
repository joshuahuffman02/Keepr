# Marketing Your Campground on Social Media

**Meta Description:** Build a social media presence that drives campground bookings. Platform strategies, content ideas, and best practices for campground marketing.

**Target Keywords:** campground social media, camping Instagram, RV park Facebook, campground marketing, social media for campgrounds

## Introduction

Social media has changed how travelers discover campgrounds. Instagram showcases stunning campsites, Facebook groups buzz with recommendations, and TikTok features RV adventures.

This guide covers how to build a social media presence that drives bookings.

---

## Choosing Platforms

### Focus Over Spread

**Priority platforms:**

| Platform | Priority | Best For |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Facebook | Essential | Local reach, events, 35+ demographic |
| Instagram | High | Visual storytelling, discovery |
| Google Business | Essential | Search visibility, reviews |

---

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

**1. Showcase (40%):** Beautiful photos, sunsets, amenities
**2. Educational (20%):** Camping tips, packing lists
**3. Community (25%):** Guest photos, staff spotlights
**4. Promotional (15%):** Offers, availability, booking reminders

### Weekly Rhythm

- Monday: Morning beauty shot
- Wednesday: Tip or educational post
- Friday: Weekend booking prompt
- Stories: Daily behind-the-scenes

---

## Creating Great Content

### Photography Tips

- Golden hour lighting
- Clean, maintained sites
- People enjoying themselves
- Wide shots and details

### Writing Captions

**Formula:**
1. Hook (grabs attention)
2. Body (story or info)
3. Call to action

---

## Engagement Strategies

- Respond to every comment
- Answer messages within 24 hours
- Repost guest content (with permission)
- Join local camping Facebook groups

---

## Converting to Bookings

- Use "link in bio" consistently
- Clear calls to action in posts
- Track how guests found you
