**Meta Description:** Build an email list that drives repeat campground bookings. Templates, automation, and strategies for effective campground email marketing.

## Introduction

Email marketing is the highest-ROI marketing channel available—and it's one campground owners often overlook. Your email list is an asset you own, full of people who've already chosen to stay with you.

---

## Building Your Email List

### Where to Collect Emails

- **At booking:** Required field in reservation
- **At check-in:** Confirm or collect if missing  
- **On website:** Newsletter signup form
- **At checkout:** "Stay in touch" invitation

### What to Promise

> "Join our email list for exclusive deals, events, and campground updates. We email monthly—never spam."

---

## Email Types That Work

### 1. Transactional Emails
- Booking confirmations
- Pre-arrival information
- Post-departure thank-you

### 2. Promotional Emails
- Seasonal rate announcements
- Last-minute availability
- Early booking access

### 3. Newsletter Emails
- Monthly campground updates
- Event announcements
- Local area news
- Camping tips

### 4. Triggered Emails
- Birthday greetings
- Anniversary of first stay
- "We miss you" for lapsed guests

## Sample Email Templates

### Pre-Arrival Email (2-3 Days Before)

```
Subject: See You Soon! Here's What to Know

Hi [Name],

We're excited to welcome you on [Date]!

CHECK-IN: [Time window] at the main office
YOUR SITE: [Number]
ADDRESS: [Full address]

WHAT TO BRING:
• Confirmation (this email!)
• Valid ID
• [Any specific items]

Questions? Reply to this email or call [phone].

See you soon!
[Campground Name]
```

### Monthly Newsletter

```
Subject: [Month] at [Campground]

WHAT'S NEW
[Brief campground update]

UPCOMING EVENTS
• [Event 1] - [Date]
• [Event 2] - [Date]

AVAILABILITY ALERT
[Specific weekends with openings]

FEATURED GUEST PHOTO
[Guest photo with credit]

Ready to book? [Link]
```

## Automation Basics

### Set Up These Sequences

**Booking confirmation:** Immediate
**Pre-arrival:** 2-3 days before
**Welcome:** Day of arrival
**Post-stay:** 1-2 days after departure
**Review request:** 3-5 days after departure
**"We miss you":** 11 months after last stay

---

## Best Practices

- **Send from a real person** (name@campground.com)
- **Mobile-friendly design** (most read on phones)
- **Clear unsubscribe option** (required by law)
- **Consistent timing** (same day/time builds expectation)
- **Test subject lines** (open rates vary significantly)

---

## Key Takeaways

- Build your list at every guest touchpoint
- Automate transactional and triggered emails
- Send monthly newsletters to stay top-of-mind
- Always include booking links and clear CTAs
