# How to Attract Millennial and Gen Z Campers

**Meta Description:** Reach younger camping audiences with updated amenities and marketing. What Millennials and Gen Z expect from campgrounds.

**Target Keywords:** attract millennial campers, Gen Z camping, young camper marketing, modern campground amenities

**Word Count:** ~1,000 words

---

## Introduction

Millennials (born 1981-1996) and Gen Z (born 1997-2012) are the future of camping. Understanding what they want helps you capture this growing market.

---

## What Younger Campers Want

### Must-Haves

| Amenity | Priority |
|---------|----------|
| Strong WiFi | Critical |
| Online booking | Essential |
| Clean facilities | Expected |
| Photos and reviews | Decision drivers |
| Instagram-worthy spots | High value |

### Nice-to-Haves
- Pet-friendly policies
- Unique experiences (glamping, unique sites)
- Sustainability practices
- Flexible cancellation
- Mobile-friendly everything

---

## Marketing to Younger Audiences

### Digital Presence
- Instagram before Facebook
- TikTok for discovery
- Respond quickly to messages
- Show real experiences, not stock photos

### Booking Experience
- Fully online booking (no phone call required)
- Mobile-optimized website
- Clear photos of actual sites
- Instant confirmation

### Communication Style
- Casual, authentic tone
- Less formal, more friendly
- Show personality
- Quick responses to questions
