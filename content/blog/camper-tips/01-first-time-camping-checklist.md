## Introduction

Planning your first camping trip? The key to a great experience is preparation. This comprehensive checklist covers everything you need—from shelter to snacks.

---

## Shelter and Sleep

### Essentials

- [ ] Tent (practice setup before you go!)
- [ ] Ground tarp (protects tent floor)
- [ ] Sleeping bag (rated for expected temps)
- [ ] Sleeping pad or air mattress
- [ ] Pillow (or stuff sack with clothes)

### Nice to Have

- [ ] Extra blankets
- [ ] Tent footprint
- [ ] Camping cot

---

## Campsite Setup

- [ ] Camp chairs (one per person)
- [ ] Camping table (or use picnic table)
- [ ] Lantern or camp lights
- [ ] Headlamp or flashlight (per person)
- [ ] Extra batteries
- [ ] Tarp for shade/rain cover

## Before You Leave Home

- [ ] Confirm reservation
- [ ] Check weather forecast
- [ ] Tell someone your plans
- [ ] Charge all devices
- [ ] Pack car strategically (last items first)
- [ ] Print campground map/directions

---

## Tips for First-Timers

1. **Arrive with daylight** — Setting up in the dark is frustrating
2. **Practice tent setup** — Do it in your backyard first
3. **Keep it simple** — Don't over-plan activities; relax!
4. **Embrace imperfection** — Something will go wrong; laugh about it
5. **Take photos** — You'll want to remember your first trip

---

## Key Takeaways

- Use a checklist to avoid forgetting essentials
- Pack for weather changes
- Practice tent setup before you go
- Arrive with daylight
- Keep your first trip simple and fun
