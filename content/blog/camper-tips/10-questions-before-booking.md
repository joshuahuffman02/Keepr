# Questions to Ask Before Booking a Campsite

**Meta Description:** Important questions to ask before reserving your campsite. Avoid surprises and ensure the campground meets your needs.

**Target Keywords:** questions before booking campsite, campground booking questions, camping reservation tips

**Word Count:** ~800 words

---

## Introduction

A few questions before booking can save you from disappointment, extra costs, or an unsuitable campsite. Here's what to ask.

---

## About the Site

- What are the actual site dimensions?
- What hookups are available?
- Is the site shaded or open?
- What is the surface (paved, gravel, grass)?
- Can I choose a specific site, or is it assigned?
- Is the site level?

---

## About Policies

### Reservations

- What is your cancellation policy?
- Is a deposit required? How much?
- When is the balance due?
- Are modifications allowed?

### Check-In/Out

- What are check-in and check-out times?
- Can I request early check-in or late checkout?
- What is the after-hours process?

### Guests

- How many guests are allowed per site?
- Are visitors permitted? At what cost?
- What are quiet hours?

---

## About Pets

- Are pets allowed?
- Any breed or size restrictions?
- Is there a pet fee?
- Are there designated pet areas?
- What are the leash rules?

---

## About Amenities

- Are restrooms/showers on-site?
- Is WiFi available? How is the quality?
- Is there a pool or lake access?
- Is firewood available for purchase?
- Are there any extra fees for amenities?

---

## About the Area

- What is nearby (groceries, restaurants)?
- What activities are available on-site?
- Is cell service reliable?
- What is the noise level like?
- Are there any current issues (construction, closures)?

---

## Before Finalizing

- What is included in the quoted price?
- Are there any additional fees (taxes, resort fees)?
- What forms of payment do you accept?
- Will I receive a confirmation email?
