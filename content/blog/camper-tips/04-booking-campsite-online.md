## Introduction

If you've never booked a campsite online, the process is straightforward. Here's what to expect from start to finish.

---

## Before You Book

### Information You'll Need

- Arrival and departure dates
- Type of site (tent, RV, cabin)
- RV length (if applicable)
- Number of guests
- Number of vehicles
- Pet information (if applicable)
- Payment method

### Good to Research First

- Campground rules and policies
- Cancellation terms
- Check-in and check-out times
- What's included vs. extra fees

---

## The Booking Process

### Step 1: Check Availability

- Enter your dates
- Select site type
- See what's available

### Step 2: Choose Your Site

- Some campgrounds assign sites
- Others let you choose specific sites
- Review site details (hookups, size, location)

### Step 3: Enter Your Information

- Contact details
- Vehicle information
- Guest count
- Any special requests

### Step 4: Review and Pay

- Confirm all details
- Review total (including taxes/fees)
- Enter payment information
- Submit booking

### Step 5: Confirmation

- Receive email confirmation
- Save or print for check-in
- Add to your calendar

---

## Common Questions

**Can I choose my specific site?**
Depends on the campground. Some allow site selection; others assign sites.

**When is payment due?**
Usually a deposit at booking, balance at check-in. Varies by campground.

**What if I need to cancel?**
Each campground has a cancellation policy. Read before booking!

**How far in advance should I book?**
Popular dates: months ahead. Less popular: days or weeks is often fine.

**Will I get a confirmation?**
Yes! Check email (and spam folder). Save or print it.

## After Booking

- Review your confirmation details
- Note check-in time and process
- Research the area and plan activities
- Watch for pre-arrival emails

## Key Takeaways

- Have your dates and party details ready
- Read the cancellation policy before booking
- Save your confirmation email
- Check for pre-arrival communication
