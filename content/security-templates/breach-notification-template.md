## Template 1: Customer Notification Letter

---

**[DATE]**

**[CUSTOMER NAME]**
**[ADDRESS]**
**[CITY, STATE ZIP]**

**RE: Notice of Data Security Incident**

Dear [Customer Name],

We are writing to inform you of a data security incident that may have affected your personal information. We take the protection of your information seriously and want to provide you with details about what happened and the steps we are taking.

### What Happened

On [DATE], we discovered [brief description of incident, e.g., "unauthorized access to our reservation system"]. We immediately [actions taken, e.g., "secured our systems and began an investigation"].

Our investigation determined that the incident occurred between [DATE] and [DATE].

### What Information Was Involved

The following types of your personal information may have been accessed:

- [ ] Name
- [ ] Address
- [ ] Email address
- [ ] Phone number
- [ ] Dates of stay
- [ ] Payment card information (last 4 digits only)
- [ ] [Other specific data types]

**[If payment cards were NOT affected]:** We do not store full credit card numbers, so your payment card information was not compromised.

### What We Are Doing

We have taken the following steps to address this incident:

1. [Action 1, e.g., "Secured affected systems and changed all passwords"]
2. [Action 2, e.g., "Engaged a cybersecurity firm to investigate"]
3. [Action 3, e.g., "Implemented additional security measures"]
4. [Action 4, e.g., "Notified law enforcement"]

### What You Can Do

We recommend you take the following precautions:

1. **Monitor your accounts** - Review your bank and credit card statements for unauthorized activity
2. **Change passwords** - If you use the same password for our system and other accounts, change those passwords
3. **Watch for phishing** - Be cautious of emails asking for personal information
4. **Consider a fraud alert** - Contact one of the three credit bureaus to place a fraud alert:
   - Equifax: 1-800-525-6285
   - Experian: 1-888-397-3742
   - TransUnion: 1-800-680-7289

### Free Credit Monitoring [If Offering]

We are offering [X months] of free credit monitoring through [SERVICE NAME]. To enroll:

1. Visit: [URL]
2. Enter code: [CODE]
3. Complete enrollment by: [DATE]

### For More Information

If you have questions, please contact us:

- **Phone:** [NUMBER]
- **Email:** [EMAIL]
- **Hours:** [HOURS]

We sincerely apologize for any concern this may cause and are committed to protecting your information.

Sincerely,

**[NAME]**
**[TITLE]**
**[CAMPGROUND NAME]**
