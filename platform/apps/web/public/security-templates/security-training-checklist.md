### Phishing Red Flags Reviewed

- [ ] Urgent/threatening language
- [ ] Requests for login credentials
- [ ] Misspelled sender domains
- [ ] Generic greetings ("Dear Customer")
- [ ] Unexpected attachments
- [ ] Too-good-to-be-true offers
- [ ] Requests to bypass normal procedures

### Quick Quiz

1. You receive an email saying "Your Campreserv account will be deleted in 24 hours - click here to verify." You should:
   - [ ] Click the link immediately to avoid losing access
   - [x] Report it as suspicious and verify directly with your manager
   - [ ] Forward it to coworkers to warn them

2. Someone calls claiming to be from "IT Support" asking for your password to "fix an issue." You should:
   - [ ] Give them the password so they can help
   - [x] Hang up and call your manager to verify
   - [ ] Ask them a security question first
