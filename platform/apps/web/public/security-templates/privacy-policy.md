## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

When you make a reservation or visit our campground, we may collect:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
- **Identification:** Driver's license number, license plate number, vehicle description
- **Reservation Details:** Dates of stay, site preferences, number of guests
- **Payment Information:** Credit/debit card details (processed securely, not stored)
- **Emergency Contact:** Name and phone number of emergency contact
- **Special Requests:** Accessibility needs, pet information, special accommodations

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website
- **Cookies:** See our Cookie Policy section below

### Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

- Online travel agencies and booking platforms
- Payment processors
- Marketing partners (with your consent)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Process Reservations:** Confirm bookings, process payments, send confirmation emails
- **Provide Services:** Check you in, assign sites, respond to requests
- **Communicate with You:** Send reservation reminders, respond to inquiries, provide customer support
- **Safety & Security:** Verify identity, maintain security records, respond to emergencies
- **Improve Services:** Analyze usage patterns, enhance guest experience
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with laws, respond to legal requests
- **Marketing:** Send promotional offers (only with your consent)
