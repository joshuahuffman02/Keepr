## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for the retention and disposal of guest data, business records, and other information collected by [Campground Name] in the course of normal business operations.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all data collected, processed, or stored by [Campground Name], including:

- Guest reservation records
- Payment transaction logs
- Employee records
- Business correspondence
- Marketing data

## 3. Retention Periods

### 3.1 Guest Records

| Data Type           | Retention Period           | Justification               |
| ------------------- | -------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Reservation details | 7 years                    | Tax/accounting requirements |
| Contact information | 3 years after last stay    | Customer service needs      |
| Payment card data   | Do not store               | PCI DSS compliance          |
| ID/license copies   | 30 days                    | Verification only           |
| Vehicle information | Duration of stay + 90 days | Security purposes           |

### 3.2 Financial Records

| Data Type            | Retention Period | Justification       |
| -------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------- |
| Transaction records  | 7 years          | IRS requirements    |
| Refund documentation | 7 years          | Dispute resolution  |
| Daily reports        | 3 years          | Business operations |

### 3.3 Employee Records

| Data Type       | Retention Period                               | Justification      |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------ |
| Personnel files | 7 years after termination                      | Legal compliance   |
| Payroll records | 7 years                                        | Tax requirements   |
| I-9 forms       | 3 years after hire or 1 year after termination | USCIS requirements |

### 3.4 Security Records

| Data Type               | Retention Period | Justification          |
| ----------------------- | ---------------- | ---------------------- |
| Security camera footage | 30 days          | Incident investigation |
| Access logs             | 90 days          | Security review        |
| Incident reports        | 7 years          | Legal/insurance        |

## 4. Data Disposal Procedures

### 4.1 Paper Records

- Cross-cut shredding required for all documents containing personal information
- Regular shredding schedule: [Weekly/Monthly]
- Shredding service provider: [Name] (if applicable)

### 4.2 Electronic Records

- Secure deletion using approved software
- Hard drive destruction for decommissioned equipment
- Cloud data: Ensure deletion from all backups

### 4.3 Disposal Log

Maintain a log of disposed records including:

- Date of disposal
- Type of records destroyed
- Method of destruction
- Person responsible

## 5. Exceptions

Extended retention may be required for:

- Ongoing litigation or legal holds
- Regulatory investigations
- Insurance claims
- Active disputes with guests

## 6. Responsibilities

| Role          | Responsibility                  |
| ------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Owner/Manager | Policy oversight, annual review |
| Office Staff  | Day-to-day compliance           |
| All Employees | Following disposal procedures   |

## 7. Annual Review

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as needed to reflect:

- Changes in legal requirements
- Business practice changes
- Industry best practices
