# Report Inventory

## /reports (Reports dashboard)

Cataloged reports available on the main reports page.

### Overview (tab: overview)

- Purpose: High-level KPIs and trends at a glance
- Reports:
  - Dashboard summary (id: dashboard-summary) - Revenue, occupancy, ADR, RevPAR

### Daily Operations (tab: daily)

- Purpose: Day-to-day arrivals, departures, and transactions
- Reports:
  - Daily summary (id: daily-summary) - Today's pickup, arrivals, departures
  - Transaction log (id: transaction-log) - Payments, refunds, charges
  - Arrivals list (id: arrivals-list) - Expected arrivals today
  - Departures list (id: departures-list) - Expected departures today
  - In-house guests (id: in-house-guests) - Currently checked-in guests
  - Housekeeping status (id: housekeeping-status) - Cleaning status by site
  - Maintenance requests (id: maintenance-daily) - Open tickets for today
  - Shift notes (id: shift-notes) - Manager log and handovers
  - Meal count (id: meal-count) - Breakfast/Dinner guest list
  - Parking & Vehicles (id: parking-list) - Registered vehicles on site
  - Pet list (id: pet-list) - Registered pets in house
  - Early check-ins (id: early-check-ins) - Guests arriving before 3PM
  - Late check-outs (id: late-check-outs) - Guests departing after 11AM
  - No-show report (id: no-show) - Missed arrivals
  - Out of order (id: out-of-order) - Sites taken offline
  - Comp & Staff stays (id: comp-stays) - Non-revenue occupied sites
  - Due outs (Overstays) (id: due-outs) - Guests past departure time
  - VIP arrivals (id: vip-arrivals) - High-value guests arriving
  - Expected occupancy (id: expected-occupancy) - Forecast for next 24h
  - Daily rate check (id: daily-rate-check) - Variance report

### Revenue (tab: revenue)

- Purpose: Detailed revenue analysis and breakdowns
- Reports:
  - Revenue overview (id: revenue-overview) - Gross/Net, ADR, RevPAR
  - By source (id: revenue-by-source) - Online vs admin vs kiosk
  - By site type (id: revenue-by-site-type) - Performance by category
  - By rate plan (id: revenue-by-rate-plan) - Standard vs Promo vs Group
  - Add-ons & POS (id: revenue-by-addon) - Firewood, ice, rentals
  - By user (id: revenue-by-user) - Staff booking performance
  - Discount usage (id: discount-usage) - Promo codes and overrides
  - Refunds & Adjustments (id: refunds-adjustments) - Rebates and fixes
  - Tax collection (id: tax-collection) - Lodging and sales tax
  - Payment methods (id: payment-methods) - Cash vs Card vs Check
  - Cash drawer (id: cash-drawer) - Shift reconciliation
  - Voided transactions (id: voided-transactions) - Cancelled payments
  - Monthly recap (id: monthly-recap) - MTD performance
  - RevPAR by type (id: revpar-site-type) - Yield efficiency
  - Ancillary analysis (id: ancillary-revenue) - Non-room revenue
  - Cancellation impact (id: cancellation-impact) - Lost revenue by cancellation

### Performance (tab: performance)

- Purpose: Site and property performance metrics
- Reports:
  - Pace vs target (id: pace) - On-the-books vs goals
  - Occupancy & ADR (id: occupancy) - Blend by date/site type
  - Site breakdown (id: site-breakdown) - RevPAR, ADR by site
  - Day of week (id: occupancy-day-week) - Midweek vs Weekend
  - Length of stay (id: los-analysis) - Average nights per stay
  - Lead time (id: lead-time) - Booking window patterns
  - Cancellations (id: cancellations) - Lost business analysis
  - Channel production (id: channel-production) - OTA vs Direct efficiency
  - Competitive set (id: comp-set) - Market comparison
  - Year-over-Year (id: yoy-performance) - Growth trends
  - Market segments (id: market-segments) - Business vs Leisure
  - Site utilization (id: site-utilization) - Hot vs Cold sites
  - Yield efficiency (id: yield-management) - Dynamic pricing impact
  - Promotions (id: promotion-perf) - Campaign lift
  - Upsell conversion (id: upsell-conversion) - Add-on attachment rate

## Other staff-facing report pages

- /reports/saved - Saved report views with quick run links and report export queue history.
- /reports/portfolio - Cross-park reporting (occupancy, ADR, RevPAR) with portfolio selection and FX conversion.
- /reports/devices - Device analytics (sessions, bookings, conversion rate) by device type over a selectable period.
- /reports/audit - Audit log with filters and CSV export (standalone view).

## Analytics dashboards (staff)

- /dashboard/analytics - Analytics dashboard covering sessions, feature usage, booking funnel performance, and anomaly summary.
- /dashboard/analytics/features - Feature adoption report with usage counts, unique users, success rate, and category breakdowns.
- /dashboard/analytics/funnels - Funnel analysis for booking, check-in, staff reservation creation, and POS flows with drop-off metrics.
- /dashboard/analytics/live - Live activity feed with real-time events, filters, and active session/device breakdown.
- /dashboard/analytics/pages - Page usage report tracking views, unique users, time on page, actions, searches, and errors.
- /dashboard/analytics/staff - Staff efficiency report with productivity, actions, errors, and reservation activity by staff member.
- /analytics - Analytics dashboard with performance metrics, revenue trends, occupancy forecast, task metrics, and export tooling.

## Staff settings reports

- /campgrounds/[campgroundId]/staff/reports - Timesheet report with summary + detailed entries, date presets, and CSV export.
- /campgrounds/[campgroundId]/reports - Report catalog runner (dynamic list from API) with categories, dimensions/metrics, chart output, and table results.

## Notes

- The report catalog runner pulls report definitions from the API at runtime, so the exact list depends on backend configuration.
